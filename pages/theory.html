Hardware Theory & Methodology

Documentation on how we test, calibrate, and evaluate the latest PC components. Based on industry standards and peer-reviewed benchmarks.

01. GPU Benchmarking Protocol

Our GPU testing suite involves 25 different titles across 1080p, 1440p, and 4K resolutions. We measure average FPS, 1% lows, and 0.1% lows to ensure frame-time consistency. All cards are tested in an open-air test bench with a controlled ambient temperature of 21°C.

"Reliability starts with consistent environmental variables. We use industrial-grade sensors to monitor power draw directly from the PCIe slot."

02. CPU Thermal Management

Thermal throttling metrics are recorded during 30-minute stress tests in Cinebench R23. We utilize high-precision liquid coolers to determine the absolute ceiling of silicon performance before heat becomes a limiting factor.
